Mapbox Studio Workshop

Spring Fling, April 2016. Presenters: Rafa Gutierrez | @geografa & Manny Lopez | @himannylopez

Welcome!

This workshop is an introduction to Mapbox Studio. It is meant to be a hands-on course working with Mapbox Studio and real-world data. Before you get started, please have the following ready:

  • Laptop with a modern browser capable of viewing WebGL
  • A Mapbox account
  • Review the CUGOS Code of Conduct

What we'll cover

Part I - Mapbox Studio Basics

  • the Mapbox platform
  • the studio interface and terms we'll use throughout (go through intro)
  • Mapbox data - OpenStreetMap, Imagery, Terrain

Part II - The Mapbox Studio Editor

  • creating a basic style from a template
  • Publishing and sharing your style

Part III - Managing your account & custom data

  • uploading a tileset
  • managing data
  • managing your account
  • using access tokens for managing styles and apps

Part IV - Advanced styling

  • filters
  • styles by zoom level
  • fonts
  • sprites
  • debugging - collision boxes, satellite imagery, tile boundaries
  • history

Part V - Final notes

  • Getting help
  • GL JS
  • Mobile Apps
  • API
